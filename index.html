STOP 6G

Brussels-based international collective advocating for a world free from embodied AI, total control and surveillance. The battle started today.

Manifesto

01. Human Sovereignty

Leading the frontline against the deployment of autonomous AI robot armies and the weaponization of embodied AI.

02. Cognitive Liberty

Protecting the human mind from algorithmic manipulation and the intrusive synchronization of neural interfaces with 6G networks.

03. The Right to Disconnect

Demanding the legal and physical right to exist outside the digital grid. Analogue life is a fundamental human right, not a luxury.

04. Anti-Total Surveillance

Opposing the "Internet of Bodies" (IoB) and the real-time biometric tracking of human biology through 6G-connected sensors.

05. Decentralized Existence

Promoting local, independent communication infrastructures that cannot be shut down or controlled by centralized technocratic entities.

06. Biological Integrity

Preserving human health and natural ecosystems against unproven high-frequency radiation and the saturation of the magnetosphere.

07. Precautionary Principle

Demanding independent scientific proof of safety before any global deployment of sub-terahertz frequencies.

08. Environmental Conscience

Opposing the massive energy consumption of AI-driven 6G networks and the destructive mining of rare minerals required for its hardware.

09. Ethical Autonomy

Refusing the outsourcing of human judgment to "black-box" AI models that lack accountability and ethical empathy.

10. Evolutionary Preservation

Defending the biological evolution of the human species against the forced transhumanist agenda and synthetic biology integration.
